It was at Millard South that Jack met his future wife, Rachel. They began dating in 2013, and on May 30th of 2019 he purposed to her. They now play on getting married in the summer of 2021 in Breckenridge, Colorado. Rachel is currently a teacher at Pine Creek Elementary in Bennington. Career and Education

While still just a junior in high school Jack duel enrolled at Metropolitan Community College and obtained a certificate of Paramedicine before graduation. During his schooling he went on ride along with various hospitals as well as fire departments. Jack's first ride along was with a fire department and the first call he went on was a man that had been stabbed, according to Jack he was hooked instantly.

He went on to become a Certified Nursing Assistant and got hired at Children’s Hospital. There he worked in the Pediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU). While working in the PICU he continued his education at the University of Nebraska Medical Center. It was there that he finished his bachelor’s degree. Shortly after becoming a registered nurse he was offered a new position at Children’s Hospital as a nurse in a cardiology unit. Jack continues to happily work at Children’s and pans on returning to school to obtain his Doctorate of Nursing Practice.
